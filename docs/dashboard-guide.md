# Dashboard Guide

This document explains the purpose of each visualization included in the **Educational Performance Dashboard** and how users can interpret the insights generated from the dashboard.

---

# Grade Distribution

### Purpose

Displays the overall distribution of grades across all student records.

### Why it matters

Provides a quick overview of institutional academic performance by showing how grades are distributed among students.

### Insight

Helps identify the most common grade categories and understand overall academic achievement.

---

# Numeric Grade Distribution

### Purpose

Shows the frequency of numeric grade values.

### Why it matters

Provides a more detailed statistical view of grade concentration and performance variation.

### Insight

Useful for identifying performance clusters and understanding overall grade trends.

---

# Failures by Subject

### Purpose

Ranks subjects according to the number of student failures.

### Why it matters

Highlights academically challenging subjects that may require curriculum review or additional academic support.

### Insight

Supports educators in identifying courses where targeted interventions can improve student success.

---

# Average Grade by Subject

### Purpose

Compares the average numeric grade achieved across all subjects.

### Why it matters

Helps evaluate subject-wise academic performance and identify both high-performing and low-performing courses.

### Insight

Useful for curriculum assessment and continuous academic improvement.

---

# Subject Credit Weight

### Purpose

Visualizes the academic credit allocation of each subject using a treemap.

### Why it matters

Shows how academic workload is distributed throughout the curriculum.

### Insight

Allows comparison between subject importance (credit weight) and student performance.

---

# Batch Distribution

### Purpose

Displays the distribution of student records across different academic batches.

### Why it matters

Helps understand enrollment patterns and compare academic trends across different student cohorts.

### Insight

Useful for institutional planning and batch-wise performance analysis.
